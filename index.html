About ARISE

ARISE stands for Advancing the Resolved Initiative to Secure and Expand. It is a five-year commitment campaign of COG Dabubu Grande that encourages every member to participate in building a lasting legacy for future generations. Through faithful giving and stewardship, members contribute toward the church's vision of expanding God's Kingdom and advancing ministry opportunities for the next generation.
